Details

We are open to new ideas and we value collaboration and partnerships. Our strategy relies heavily on successful partnerships around the world that will help us to improve our research and clinical practice.

We welcome students and doctoral fellows to our exciting research activities. We continuously offer Master Thesis opportunities for undergraduate students in our  Mater of Science in Biomedical Engineering and exciting research projects for our Doctorate of Philosophy in the Universitat Politècnica de València that help students to pursue careers in research, teaching, or industry. Postdoctoral fellows and associates are of utmost important in our group, contributing to the depth and breadth of our work.
